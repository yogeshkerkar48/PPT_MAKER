1.Paris, France – Known as the “City of Love,” famous for the Eiffel Tower, museums, art, and romantic atmosphere.

2.Rome, Italy – Home to ancient landmarks like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City.

3.New York City, USA – A global city famous for Times Square, Statue of Liberty, Broadway, and skyscrapers.

4.London, United Kingdom – Known for historical sites like Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, and rich cultural heritage.

5.Dubai, UAE – Famous for modern architecture, luxury shopping, Burj Khalifa, and desert safaris.

6.Tokyo, Japan – A blend of tradition and technology, known for temples, anime culture, and advanced city life.

7.Sydney, Australia – Famous for Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge, and beautiful beaches.

8.Santorini, Greece – Known for white-washed buildings, blue domes, and stunning sunset views.

9.Bali, Indonesia – Popular for tropical beaches, temples, rice terraces, and peaceful retreats.

10.Maldives – Famous for crystal-clear waters, overwater villas, coral reefs, and luxury resorts.

11.Barcelona, Spain – Known for unique architecture by Gaudí, beaches, and vibrant street life.

12.Cape Town, South Africa – Famous for Table Mountain, scenic coastlines, and natural beauty.

13.Istanbul, Turkey – A city connecting Europe and Asia, known for mosques, bazaars, and history.

14.Machu Picchu, Peru – An ancient Incan city set in the Andes Mountains and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

15.Bangkok, Thailand – Known for temples, street food, nightlife, and cultural experiences.

16.Venice, Italy – Famous for canals, gondola rides, historic buildings, and romantic charm.

17.Swiss Alps, Switzerland – Known for snow-capped mountains, skiing, scenic train journeys, and natural beauty.

18.Rio de Janeiro, Brazil – Famous for Christ the Redeemer statue, beaches, and Carnival festival.

19.Singapore – Known for cleanliness, modern skyline, Gardens by the Bay, and diverse food culture.

20.Petra, Jordan – An ancient rock-carved city and UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for its unique architecture.
